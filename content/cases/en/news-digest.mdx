    type: text
    body: "Following a topic means opening 10-15 sources every day, scanning headlines, deciding what's worth reading, reading it, and synthesizing it into something useful. That's 1-2 hours of work for maybe 10 minutes of actual insight. Most people either stop following entirely or drown in tabs. The information exists. The bottleneck is processing it."

  - title: "How the pipeline works"
    type: text
    screenshot: "/cases/news-digest/workflow-parser.png"
    body: "Schedule fires every 12 hours. The parser reads RSS sources from Airtable, fetches each feed, filters to articles published in the last 24 hours, and deduplicates against every URL ever processed. Surviving articles (up to 8) go to ScrapingBee which extracts the full article text — not RSS snippets, the real content. Cleaned and aggregated, they get handed off to the writer workflow."
